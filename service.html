Services offered by us

Discounts and Offers - Swiggy frequently offers discounts and deals on food orders to attract customers.

Customer Reviews - Customers can leave reviews on the food and delivery service, which helps other customers make informed decisions.

Gift Cards - Swiggy allows customers to purchase gift cards for their friends and family.

Subscription Services - Swiggy offers subscription services for frequent customers, which includes exclusive offers and discounts.

Express Delivery - Swiggy provides express delivery service in certain areas, which guarantees delivery within 30 minutes.

Multiple Language Support - Swiggy's app and website support multiple languages, making it accessible to a wider audience.
